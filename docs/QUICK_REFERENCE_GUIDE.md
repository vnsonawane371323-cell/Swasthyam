### Daily Oil Budget Formula

```
Your Budget = (BMR × Activity Level × 0.07) kcal

Step 1: Know Your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate)
- Use app's onboarding calculator
- Or: Weight × 10 + Height × 6.25 - Age × 5 ± Gender

Step 2: Apply Activity Level
- Sedentary (no exercise): × 1.2
- Light (1-3 days/week): × 1.375
- Moderate (3-5 days/week): × 1.55 ← Most common
- Very Active (6-7 days/week): × 1.725
- Extra Active (job + exercise): × 1.9

Step 3: Calculate Daily Budget
- Multiply by 0.07 (ICMR 7% recommendation)

EXAMPLE: 75kg man, 28 years, 175cm, moderately active
BMR = 1674 kcal
TDEE = 1674 × 1.55 = 2594 kcal
Oil Budget = 2594 × 0.07 = 182 kcal/day ≈ 20g ≈ 22ml
```

### Calorie Calculations

**Raw Oil Calories:**
```
Any oil = 9 kcal per gram
Example: 20g of any oil = 180 kcal
```

**Quality Adjustment (Harm Score):**
```
Harm Score = (SFA% × 0.35) + (TFA% × 0.40) + (PUFA% × 0.25)
Higher score = More health penalty
SwasthaIndex = 100 - Harm Score
```

**Cooking Method Impact:**
```
Final Volume = Base Volume × Method Factor

Example:
200ml Mustard Oil × 1.15 (Shallow Fry) = 230ml final
(230 - 200 = 30ml extra absorbed)
```

### IoT Tracker Connection (Quick)

**Setup (5 minutes):**
1. Scale on WiFi + powered on
2. App → Settings → IoT Devices
3. Tap "Add Device" → Enter IP address
4. Tap "Connect"
5. Calibrate: Place 1kg weight on scale

**Using:**
1. Place oil on scale
2. App shows reading automatically
3. Select: Cooking method, Reuse count, Oil type
4. Tap "Log" → See feedback instantly

---

### Medical Report Upload (Quick)

**Steps:**
1. Take photo/PDF of medical report
2. App → Profile → Medical Reports → Upload
3. Wait 1-2 minutes (AI analyzes)
4. See Health Score + Oil Recommendation

**What App Extracts:**
```
✅ Cholesterol levels (total, LDL, HDL)
✅ Diabetes markers (HbA1c, glucose)
✅ Liver function (ALT, AST, bilirubin)
✅ Kidney function (creatinine, urea)
✅ Blood count (hemoglobin, platelets)
✅ Vitamins (B12, D)
✅ Minerals (iron, calcium)
```

### Recommendations Summary

#### For Healthy People:
- **Daily Budget**: 20-30ml (use calculator)
- **Preferred Methods**: Boil, Saute
- **Best Oils**: Mustard, Sunflower, Olive
- **Frequency**: Use daily, track weekly

#### For High Cholesterol:
- **Daily Budget**: 15ml or less (reduce 30%)
- **Preferred Methods**: Boil, Saute only
- **Best Oils**: Olive, Mustard
- **Avoid**: Ghee, Coconut (strictly limit)
- **Action**: Upload medical report for personalized limit

#### For Diabetics:
- **Daily Budget**: 16-20ml (reduce 20%)
- **Preferred Methods**: Boil, Saute
- **Best Oils**: Olive, Mustard
- **Frequency**: Daily consistent tracking
- **Medical**: Upload report, follow doctor

#### For Fatty Liver:
- **Daily Budget**: 10ml or less (reduce 50%)
- **Preferred Methods**: Boil only
- **Best Oils**: Olive, small amounts Mustard
- **Avoid**: Ghee, Coconut completely
- **Medical**: Must follow doctor's advice

### Pro Tips for Success

**1. Cooking Method Tips:**
- Saute is your friend (uses 5% more oil only)
- Batch cook to control oil usage
- Use measured spoon (not pouring directly)

**2. Selection Tips:**
- Start with Mustard oil (best for Indian cooking)
- Keep extra Olive oil for premium meals
- Avoid Ghee for regular meals (special only)

**3. Tracking Tips:**
- Log immediately after cooking (don't forget)
- Use IoT scale for precision
- Round up if unsure (safety margin)

**4. Health Tips:**
- Pair oil with fiber (reduces fat absorption)
- Use high heat (oil breaks down less)
- Filter and store properly (extends life)

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Mistake**: Skipping logs
✅ **Solution**: Log immediately, use IoT for auto-logging

❌ **Mistake**: Using same oil >3 times
✅ **Solution**: Discard after 3 uses, especially sunflower

❌ **Mistake**: Ignoring reuse count
✅ **Solution**: Each reuse reduces oil quality 5%

❌ **Mistake**: Not updating health profile
✅ **Solution**: Update annually or after medical checkup

❌ **Mistake**: Guessing cooking method
✅ **Solution**: Ask device owner or best guess from list
